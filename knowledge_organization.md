Evolution of knowledge storage  
- Individual biological storage  
- Spoken Language  
  - Oral history/mythology  
- Written Language  
  - Cave Paintings  
  - Personal journal  
  - Book / Novel / Epic Poem  
  - Peer reviewed scientific journal  
  - etc...  
- Digital Storage  
  - Databases  
  - visualizations  
  - etc...
